    Still, it is foreseeable that, as Ethereum consolidates its position as a platform for smart contracts and as a cryptocurrency, criminals will exploit it to host their scams. Great, now don't be scared by all of the jargon! Here are a few key words to get you started.

  • Decentralized - computers distributed all over the world, working together autonomously, if half of the comptuers are turned off, the other half will continue operating as if nothing happened.
  • Cryptocurrency - a kind of money that derrives it's value from having computers do hard math problems and then telling the other computers in the network about it's solution.
  • Computing Platform - this is a fancy way of saying a distributed network of computers
